1 Overview

MoneyPrinter ("we", "our", or "the application") is a personal, locally-hosted video generation tool. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the application, including when it interacts with the TikTok API on your behalf.

Short version: MoneyPrinter runs locally on your machine. We do not operate servers, collect your data, or share anything with third parties beyond what is strictly required to post content to TikTok and YouTube on your behalf.

2 Information We Collect

The application collects and processes the following data locally on your device:

  • TikTok OAuth tokens — access token and refresh token stored locally in your .env file to authenticate API requests
  • Job data — video topics, generated scripts, file paths, and job status stored in a local SQLite or PostgreSQL database
  • Generated video files — temporary and final .mp4 files stored on your local filesystem
  • API keys — Pexels, TikTok, AssemblyAI, and YouTube credentials stored in your local .env file

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of this data on external servers operated by us.

3 How We Use Your Information

Data processed by the application is used solely for:

  • Generating video scripts via locally-running AI models (Ollama)
  • Converting scripts to speech via the TikTok TTS API
  • Searching and downloading stock footage via the Pexels API
  • Composing and rendering the final video on your device
  • Uploading the finished video to your TikTok and/or YouTube account when you choose to do so

4 TikTok API Data Usage

MoneyPrinter uses the TikTok Content Posting API. By authorizing the application, you grant it permission to:

  • Upload videos to your TikTok account on your behalf (video.publish, video.upload scopes)
  • Read basic account information to confirm authentication status

We do not access, store, or share: your TikTok followers, messages, feed, liked videos, or any personal profile data beyond what is returned by the authentication handshake.

Your TikTok OAuth tokens are stored only on your local device and are never transmitted to any server operated by MoneyPrinter. Token refresh happens directly between your device and TikTok's servers.

MoneyPrinter's use of TikTok API data complies with the TikTok API Developer Data Usage Policy.

5 Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored locally on your own machine or self-hosted server
  • Credentials are stored in a .env file that you control — keep this file private and out of version control
  • Temporary video files are automatically cleaned up after each job completes
  • We recommend running the application behind a firewall and not exposing it to the public internet

6 Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party. The only outbound data flows are:

  • TikTok — video content and metadata you explicitly choose to publish
  • YouTube — video content and metadata you explicitly choose to publish
  • Pexels — search queries to find stock footage (no personal data)
  • AssemblyAI — audio files for subtitle transcription, if configured (subject to AssemblyAI's own privacy policy)

7 Your Rights

Because all data is stored locally on your own device, you have full control at all times:

  • Access: inspect your local database or .env file at any time
  • Deletion: delete your local database, generated files, or the entire application to remove all data
  • Revocation: revoke TikTok or YouTube OAuth access at any time from your account settings on those platforms

8 Cookies

MoneyPrinter is a locally-hosted application and does not use cookies or browser tracking of any kind. The TikTok sessionid cookie used for TTS is read from your own browser session and stored only in your local .env file.

9 Children's Privacy

MoneyPrinter is intended for use by individuals aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect or process data from children under 13. The application should not be used to create or upload content targeting children without appropriate compliance with applicable laws (COPPA, etc.).

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the application after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
